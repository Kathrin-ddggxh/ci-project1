Welcome to Lowland Pride youth group! We are a community of young LGBTQ+ people who all live in and around the Burren Lowland Region.
Have a look at what we get up to!

A Safe Space

The main aim of this group is to create and provide a safe space for young people between 14 and 25 who identify as members of the LGBTQ+ community. Whether you need to talk, want to listen or just hang out, the Lowland Pride group offers a supportive environment free of judgement or prejudice. We have a firm set of rules in place to ensure everybody feels comfortable and happy in our group.

New Connections

Having a support network is important. And sometimes we need those around us who share in the same experiences we have. At Lowland Pride we are trying to bring together members of a rurally dispersed young community who are looking for like-minded people. Connect with others who can relate to you and vice versa.

Having the Craic

All talk and no fun makes young Pride members dull people. Whatever we're dealing with in life, being able to relax and have fun with friends helps us get through it. That's why we make sure we get in plenty of laughs. Apart from our weekly hangout meet-ups, we organise events and outings throughout the year to blow out the cobwebs. Head over to our events page to find out more.
