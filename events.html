Our Weeklies

After-School Hangout

Every first and third Wednesday of the month the club space will be open for a casual hanging out.

Saturday Social

Friendly afternoon sessions every second and forth Saturday of the month. For those, we often organise game days, discussion rounds or advice and support shares.

Our Specials

Throughout the year the Lowland Pride hosts or joins different LGBTQ+ events.

  • Galway Pride Festival:

    Once a year we travel up to Galway City together to view/join our local Pride parade.

  • Pizza and Game night:

    Yes, that! We get a big stack of pizzas, people bring along their favourite board games and off we go.

  • Parent Sharing Circle:

    As members feel a desire for this, we'll arrange a talking round in which parents can join. The motto for these sessions is "Help me understand, so I can support you better."

  • Ask us anything Day:

    Let's include all of our community. We'll open our doors to anybody who has questions about all things LGBTQ+. Because understanding fosters inclusion.

  • Field Trips:

    We need time to relax and not be serious. So we find a fun place we want to go to and head out into the world together.
