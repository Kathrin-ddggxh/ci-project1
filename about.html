About Us

Find out more!

Here's a little overview of what Lowland Pride is all about. No doubt there's many more questions you might have and we'd be happy to answer any of them. Send us a message over on contact.

Who

Our colourful group started out in 2022. Since then we've been welcoming all members of the LGBTQ+ community (age 14-25) from the Slieve Aughties to the Burren Highlands and from Kinvara to Crusheen. Of course anybody living outside these areas is welcome to join!

Why

Growing up can be challenging. Growing up as part of a minority group even more so. Especially for those of us who live in sparcely populated areas. Finding others who face the same challenges as us may seem difficult. This is why Lowland Pride aims to bring those together who share a connection.

What

During our regular meet-ups we share a space where we can all feel safe and unjudged while having fun and discovering ourselves. Be it during our sharing sessions, our game nights or special events and outings. Feel free to discuss whatever's bugging you. Suggest a cool activity the group could do together. And ask as many questions as you need without fear of prejudice.
Head over to our events page to find out what we do.

Where

As our great hangout spot serves the Gort Youth Cafe, located on Church Street in Gort. The cozy space is perfect for after-school relaxing, Saturday afternoon game sessions and all time round chatting. Check out our contact page for information on lift sharing to Gort.

Club Rules

To ensure that everybody feels welcome at Lowland Pride, we all adhere to the following rules:

  • No Alcohol. No Drugs.
  • No questioning of peoples feelings and experiences.
    Exp.:
    "Are you even sure you're X?"
    "Do you know what being X feels like?"
  • No sharing of NSFW/18+ content.
  • No enforcing of false standards and stereotypes.
    Exp.:
    "You don't really look X though."
    "Shouldn't you dress differently if you were X?"
  • Consent is paramount!
    This does not only apply to physical interaction. Nobody will be pressured into sharing or disussing a subject they are not comfortable to do so.
  • Be kind and respectful!
    We are here to support each other.
